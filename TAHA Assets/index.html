Company Overview

Taha Floor (Pty) Ltd (2017/516535/07) is a specialist flooring company offering high-quality, durable, and stylish floor finishes for residential, commercial, and industrial environments. Skilled professionals use advanced techniques and high-grade materials to ensure long-lasting, seamless floors.
